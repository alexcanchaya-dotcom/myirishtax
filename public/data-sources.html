Sources

Data sources

Rates, thresholds, and reliefs are pulled from public Irish Revenue materials and Department of Finance publications. We refresh the calculator and guides when new budgets or Finance Acts change the numbers.

Primary references

  • Revenue eBriefs and Tax and Duty Manuals for PAYE, USC, PRSI, and BIK.
  • Budget press releases and Finance Bill/Act schedules for updated thresholds.
  • Department of Social Protection publications for PRSI classes and credits.
  • Pensions Authority guidance for contribution limits and earnings caps.

How we track changes

  • Monitor Budget Day announcements and Revenue updates for the upcoming tax year.
  • Note effective dates and transition rules in each explainer so you can align payroll, marketing, and filings.
  • Record last updated dates on calculators and content for auditability.
