Rates overview

Key Irish tax bands

Reference the latest PAYE, USC, and PRSI notes before filing or running ads.

USC bands (illustrative)

  • 0.5% on income up to €12,012
  • 2% on €12,013 – €25,460
  • 4.5% on €25,461 – €55,216
  • 8% on remaining income

Confirm thresholds with Revenue before advertising or filing. See the USC explainer.

PRSI reminder

Most Class A employees pay 4% PRSI. Self-employed use Class S with additional treatment.

Ensure exemptions and credits are applied for part-year or low-income cases. Review the PRSI guide.

Credits & allowances

Personal + PAYE credits often total €3,500. Add home carer, age, or rent credit where eligible.

Keep evidence for claims and disclose assumptions in ad copy. See pension relief notes and BIK treatment.
