Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2025

My Irish Tax (“we”) provides an online calculator, explainers, and support options. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data in line with GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive.

Information we collect

  • Contact information (name, email, phone) submitted via forms or support channels.
  • Service context (selected service type, questions asked) so we can respond accurately.
  • Tax and employment documents you upload when you explicitly opt in to onboarding.
  • Usage and analytics data collected only when you consent via the cookie banner.
  • Consent logs stored locally in your browser showing the timestamp and categories you chose.

How we use data

  • Provide calculators, reminders, onboarding, and advisory services you request.
  • Send onboarding or reminder emails when you opt in and allow us to contact you.
  • Improve our website and guard against abuse or fraud.
  • Meet legal and tax obligations, including anti-money-laundering checks when engaged.

Legal bases

Performance of a contract or steps prior to entering one; legitimate interests (service improvement and security); compliance with legal obligations; and consent for analytics, marketing, or optional performance monitoring.

Data sharing

We do not sell data. We may share data with processors such as email delivery, secure file storage, or analytics providers bound by confidentiality and GDPR-compliant agreements. Where services are outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.

Retention

Contact enquiries are retained for up to 24 months; onboarding and engagement records follow statutory tax/audit timelines; consent logs stay in your browser until you delete them. You may request deletion where applicable.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Infrastructure is designed to support encrypted storage (e.g., SSE for document storage), role-based access, and least-privilege service accounts.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability. You may also object to processing based on legitimate interests. Contact privacy@myirishtax.com to exercise rights.

Cookies and consent

We use essential cookies plus optional analytics and performance monitoring. Manage preferences via the banner on our site, the “Cookie settings” button, or visit the Cookie Notice for details on categories and providers.
